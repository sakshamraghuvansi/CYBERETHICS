Intellectual Property Rights

Intellectual Property Rights are the legal rights given to creators and inventors to protect their original works, inventions, designs, symbols, and artistic creations. These rights allow the creator to control how their work is used by others, ensuring they receive recognition and economic benefit from their creations.

Malware


Malware is any software intentionally designed to damage computers, steal data, disrupt services, or gain unauthorized access. It is a major concern in cybersecurity.

How malware spreads-

  • Email attachments and phishing links
  • Fake software/downloads
  • Cracked/pirated programs
  • Malicious websites
  • USB and external devices
  • Exploiting unpatched vulnerabilities

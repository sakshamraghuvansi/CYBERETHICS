Cyber Laws

Cyber laws are legal regulations that control online activities to protect users from cybercrimes. They deal with data security, privacy, e-commerce, intellectual property, and cyber offenses. In India, cyber cases are handled under the IT Act 2000 which includes sections for hacking, fraud, identity theft, privacy breach, and publishing illegal content.

Objectives of Cyber Laws:

  1. To prevent and punish cybercrimes
  2. To protect privacy and personal data
  3. To regulate online transactions and e-commerce
  4. To maintain digital security and trust
  5. To safeguard intellectual property on the internet
  6. To ensure responsible behavior in cyberspace

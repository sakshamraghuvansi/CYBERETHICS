Goals of cyberethics
  1. To ensure responsible and safe use of technology.
  2. To protect privacy and personal information.
  3. To prevent cybercrimes and unethical online activities.
  4. To promote digital respect and good online behavior.
  5. To maintain integrity and honesty in cyberspace.
  6. To create awareness about cyber laws and rights.
  7. To provide a safe and secure digital environment.
  8. To encourage ethical development and use of technology.

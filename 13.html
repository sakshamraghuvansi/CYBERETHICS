Digital Divide

Digital Divide is the gap between people with access to digital technology and those without. It is caused by factors like low income, lack of literacy, poor infrastructure, and social differences. It leads to unequal opportunities in education, jobs, healthcare, and communication. Reducing it requires affordable internet, digital literacy, better infrastructure, and supportive government initiatives.

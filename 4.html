Netiquettes-Key principles of cyberethics

Netiquettes are a set of rules and good practices that guide how people should behave while communicating or interacting on the internet.
They help maintain respect, clarity, politeness, and safety in online communication.

Important Netiquettes
  1. Be polite and respectful online.
  2. Use appropriate language — avoid abusive or offensive words.
  3. Think before you post or send messages.
  4. Do not spread fake news, rumors, or misinformation.
  5. Respect others' privacy — do not share personal data without consent.
  6. Avoid spamming or sending repeated unwanted messages.
  7. Use proper grammar and clear communication.
  8. Be mindful of tone — words can be misinterpreted online.
  9. Do not plagiarize — give credit when using others' content.
  10. Avoid cyberbullying, trolling, or harassment.
  11. Respect intellectual property and copyrights.
  12. Report harmful or abusive online behavior when necessary.

Online Fraud

Online fraud is a cybercrime where criminals use the internet to deceive people and steal money, personal information, or digital assets. It involves tricking users through fake websites, emails, messages, or online transactions.
Types of online fraud:-

Phishing-
Fake emails/messages attempt to steal passwords, bank details, or OTPs.
Vishing-
Voice call phishing where attackers pretend to be from banks or government.
Smishing-
Fraudulent SMS containing malicious links or scam messages.
Credit/Debit Card Fraud
Cloned cards, stolen card details & unauthorized transactions.
Lottery/Reward Scam
“You won ₹10 lakh, pay processing fee to claim.”
Investment & Trading Scam
Fake stock/crypto schemes promising high returns quickly. >
Identity Theft
Criminals misuse personal details to open bank/loan accounts.

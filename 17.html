Cyberethics for e-commerce

Cyberethics for e-commerce refers to the ethical rules and responsible practices that online businesses and customers must follow while buying or selling products over the internet. Since e-commerce involves digital payments, personal data, and online communication, ethical behavior is essential to maintain trust, safety, and fairness.

Key points :
  1. Privacy Protection
    2.
    • E-commerce platforms must protect customers' personal information (address, phone number, payment details).
    • No unauthorized sharing or selling of customer data.
  2. Honesty and Transparency
    3.
    • Provide correct product descriptions, real images, genuine reviews.
    • Prices, taxes, delivery charges, and offers must be clearly mentioned.
  3. Consumer Rights Protection
    4.
    • Provide easy return, refund, replacement, and warranty policies.
    • Offer customer support for issues regarding orders or payments.
  4. Accessibility & Inclusivity
    5.
    • Websites should be easy to use for all, including people with disabilities.

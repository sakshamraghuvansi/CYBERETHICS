What is cyberethics?


Cyber Ethics refers to the set of moral principles, standards and guidelines that govern how individualsshould behave while using computers, the internet, and digital technologies. It tells us what is right or wrong in the online world.

Key points of cyber ethics:
  1. Respect Others Online
    • Dont bully, harass, or threaten people on the internet.
    • Avoid spreading hate, rumors, or misinformation.
  2. Protect Privacy
    • Dont access others personal data without permission.
    • Never share someones private information publicly.

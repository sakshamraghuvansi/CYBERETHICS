Cyber Addiction

Cyber Addiction (Internet Addiction) is the excessive and uncontrollable use of digital devices such as smartphones, computers, social media, gaming platforms, and the internet. A person becomes overly dependent on online activities, which affects their daily life, productivity, behavior, relationships, and health.
It is considered a behavioral disorder when online usage interferes with normal routines like study, sleep, work, or social interaction.
Effects of cyber addiction:

  1. Physical Effects:
    • Headache, eye strain, poor posture
    • Sleep problems, fatigue
    • Reduced physical activity leading to health issues
  2. Social/Academic Effects:
    • Poor academic or work performance
    • Less time for family and friends
    • Neglect of responsibilities
